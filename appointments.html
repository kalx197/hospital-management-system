Appointment FAQ

How do I cancel or reschedule my appointment?

You can cancel or reschedule your appointment by calling our appointment desk at (251)9 123-45678 at least 24 hours before your scheduled time.

What should I bring to my appointment?

Please bring your ID, insurance card (if applicable), list of current medications, and any previous medical records related to your condition.

How early should I arrive for my appointment?

Please arrive 15 minutes before your scheduled appointment time to complete any necessary paperwork.

What if I need urgent care but can't get an appointment?

For urgent medical needs, please visit our Emergency Department which is open 24/7, or call our emergency hotline at 991 or 9207.
